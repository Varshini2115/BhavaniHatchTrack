CLEANING INSTRUCTIONS

Tank Cleaning

Follow these steps to clean the tanks:

  1. Remove all shrimp and water from the tank.
  2. Scrub the tank walls and bottom using a soft brush.
  3. Rinse the tank thoroughly with clean water.
  4. Disinfect the tank with an approved disinfectant.
  5. Allow the tank to dry completely before refilling.

Equipment Cleaning

Clean equipment regularly:

  • Wash nets and filters with clean water and mild soap.
  • Disinfect equipment using a suitable disinfectant.

DISINFECTANT INSTRUCTIONS

Proper disinfection is essential in a shrimp hatchery to maintain a clean and disease-free environment.

Recommended Disinfectants

  • Chlorine-based disinfectants: Effective against a broad range of pathogens. Use at recommended concentrations and follow safety guidelines.
  • Iodophor compounds: Suitable for disinfecting equipment and tanks. Ensure proper rinsing after disinfection.
  • Quaternary ammonium compounds (quats): Effective against bacteria and viruses. Dilute as per manufacturer's instructions.

Disinfection Procedures

  1. Thoroughly clean surfaces and equipment to remove organic matter.
  2. Prepare disinfectant solution at the recommended concentration.
  3. Apply the disinfectant evenly, ensuring complete coverage.
  4. Allow sufficient contact time as per disinfectant instructions.
  5. Rinse surfaces and equipment thoroughly with clean water.
  6. Air dry or use clean, disposable towels for drying.
